## What is Ainick

**Ainick** is a comprehensive corporate governance and company secretarial management platform designed specifically for Kenyan businesses.

### Purpose

Ainick helps businesses:
- Manage multiple companies from one dashboard
- Track compliance deadlines (Annual Returns, AGMs, BO filings)
- Generate corporate documents (resolutions, minutes, notices)
- Maintain statutory registers (directors, shareholders, beneficial owners)
- Collaborate with team members
- Stay compliant with Kenyan company law

### Key Features

- **Multi-company management**: Handle unlimited companies
- **Compliance tracking**: Automated reminders for CR29, BOF1, AGMs
- **Document generation**: Templates for all corporate documents
- **Team collaboration**: Internal messaging and notifications
- **AI Assistant**: Get instant answers on BRS Kenya and compliance
- **Audit trail**: Track all changes and actions
- **Offline capability**: Work without internet, sync later

## Companies Management

### Adding a New Company

**Steps**:
1. Click "Companies" in main menu
2. Click "Add Company" button
3. Fill in company details:
   - Company name
   - Registration number
   - Date of incorporation
   - Registered office address
   - Company type (Private Ltd, LLP, Business Name, Foreign)
   - Share capital
   - Financial year end
4. Add directors with full details
5. Add shareholders with ownership percentages
6. Add beneficial owners (if 10%+ ownership)
7. Click "Save Company"

### Company Dashboard

Each company has its own dashboard with tabs:

**Overview Tab**: Company details, registration info, compliance status

**Directors Tab**: List of directors, add/remove functionality

**Shareholders Tab**: Shareholder register, share distribution

**Beneficial Owners Tab**: BO register (10%+ owners)

**Documents Tab**: All company documents and certificates

**Compliance Tab**: Annual return status, AGM requirements, filing deadlines

### Company Actions

**Add Director**: Complete details, system reminds to file CR2 within 14 days (KES 650)

**Remove Director**: Enter resignation date, system reminds to file CR8 within 14 days (KES 650)

**Transfer Shares**: Record transfer, system reminds to file CR9 within 30 days (KES 650)

**Update Beneficial Ownership**: System alerts if 10% threshold crossed, reminds to file BOF1 within 30 days

## Documents Management

### Document Types

1. **Annual Return (CR29)** - Filed annually, KES 1,000
2. **Director Resignation Letter** - Formal resignation document
3. **AGM Notice** - Notice of Annual General Meeting
4. **Board Minutes** - Record of board meetings
5. **Shareholder Resolutions** - Ordinary and special resolutions
6. **Financial Statements** - Annual financial reports
7. **Share Certificates** - Issued to shareholders
8. **Board Resolutions** - Specific board decisions

### Generating Documents

1. Click "Documents" > "Generate Document"
2. Select document type
3. Select company
4. Fill in details
5. Preview document
6. Save and download
7. Share with stakeholders

### Document Templates

**Available Templates**:
- AGM Notice
- Board Meeting Minutes
- Director Appointment/Resignation Letters
- Shareholder Resolutions
- Share Transfer Forms
- Board Resolutions
- Financial Statements

**Custom Templates**: Create your own with placeholders like {{company_name}}, {{director_names}}, {{date}}

### Roles

#### 1. Super Admin
- Full system access
- Manage all companies
- Add/remove users
- Configure system settings
- Access audit logs

#### 2. Admin
- Manage assigned companies
- Add/edit company information
- Generate documents
- Add/remove staff users

#### 3. Accountant
- View financial information

## Common Q&A

### Q1: How do I add a new company?
**A**: Click "Companies" > "Add Company", fill in details (name, registration number, directors, shareholders), and save. System automatically sets up compliance tracking.

### Q2: How does Ainick remind me about annual returns?
**A**: Ainick sends notifications 30 days before CR29 due date via email, in-app, and SMS. Dashboard shows countdown timer.

### Q3: Can I work offline?
**A**: Yes! Enable offline mode in Settings. Select companies to sync, work offline, and changes auto-sync when online.

### Q4: What happens when I add a director?
**A**: System updates director register, reminds you to file CR2 within 14 days (KES 650), generates appointment letter, and notifies team.

### Q5: How do I customize templates?
**A**: Go to "Templates" > "Create Template". Use placeholders like {{company_name}} for dynamic data. Save and reuse for any company.

### Q6: How does the AI Assistant help?
**A**: Ask questions about BRS Kenya procedures, fees, deadlines, and get instant answers with step-by-step guidance.

### Q7: What's the difference between Admin and Staff?
**A**: Admin can add/edit companies and manage users. Staff can only view companies and generate basic documents.

### Q8: How do I track beneficial ownership?
**A**: Use "Beneficial Owners" tab. Add anyone with 10%+ ownership. System alerts when BOF1 filing needed (within 30 days).

### Q9: Can multiple users work simultaneously?
**A**: Yes! System supports real-time collaboration and detects conflicts if same data edited simultaneously.

### Q10: How does Ainick handle the 14-Day Rule?
**A**: System tracks all changes and starts 14-day countdown. Sends notifications at 14, 7, 3, and 1 day before deadline.
